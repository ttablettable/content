<p>Haven't we been here before?</p><p>in this exact same situation.</p><p>I chase you; Streets. Tunnel vision.</p><p>You resist because</p><p>it helps your ego.</p><p> </p><p>I feel as though I had a soul mate</p><p>and I forgot them.</p><p> </p><p>Whoever it is, I miss our</p><p>fun times;</p><p>adventures,</p><p>projects,</p><p>enthusiasms,</p><p>unexpected visits,</p><p>a sense of possibility in every moment,</p><p>as though we could cross oceans.</p><p> </p><p>“Have a ride if you like</p><p>on my scooter”</p><p> </p><p>With one foot</p><p>placed firm on</p><p>the scooter</p><p>the other</p><p>pushed away</p><p>the hard ground.</p><p> </p><p>Saying yes was always her.</p><p>That was her thing.</p><p>And I used to laugh</p><p>because it was so</p><p>pleasing.</p><p> </p><p>Because I liked it.</p><p> </p><p>I didn’t need an answer</p><p>but I asked her why</p><p>once.</p><p> </p><p>She said she didn't know.</p><p>It was just something</p><p>she did.</p><p> </p><p>That scooter</p><p>and I have something</p><p>in common.</p><p>We travel too fast.</p><p>Too fast to fall in love.</p><p>The head over</p><p>heels type love.</p><p> </p><p>Except with her.</p><p> </p>
